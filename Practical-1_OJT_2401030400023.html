Types of Websites

  1. Static
    • Advantages
      1. Superior Speed and Performance
      2. Enhanced Security
      3. Easy SEO Implementation
    • Disadvantages
      1. Manual Maintenance
      2. Limited Interactivity
      3. Poor Scalability
  2. Dynamic
    • Advantages
      1. Easy Content Management
      2. Personalization and Interaction
      3. Enhanced Functionality
    • Disadvantages
      1. Slower Load Times
      2. Technical Complexity
      3. Higher Development Costs
